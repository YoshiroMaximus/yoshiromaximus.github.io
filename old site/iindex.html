🎨 Principles of Art and Design

  • Balance ⚖️: Achieving visual harmony by distributing elements evenly across the canvas. Every component plays a crucial role in the overall composition.
  • Contrast 🎨: Utilizing differences in color, shape, and size to highlight important elements and create visual interest.
  • Emphasis 🔍: Drawing attention to key areas of my designs, ensuring that the viewer's eye is guided smoothly across the page.
  • Movement 🚶‍♂️: Creating a sense of motion to lead viewers through my work seamlessly, making the experience dynamic and engaging.
  • Pattern 🌀: Incorporating repeating elements to build consistency and rhythm, enhancing the aesthetic appeal.
  • Unity 🌐: Ensuring all parts of my design work together cohesively to create a harmonious and functional whole.
